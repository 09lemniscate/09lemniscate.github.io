Avis

India's first aviation training academy that provides scholarships to all aspiring students who want to pursue their career in the aviation & hospitality industry. This scheme opens up future career paths for students allowing them to study the course at reduced fees. This economic assistance would help students to fulfill their dreams into reality.

the modern age aviation training academy where we not only provide aviation knowledge, but also work towards building confident personalities to meet today ‘s challenges of the aviation & hospitality industry.
